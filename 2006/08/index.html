27th Aug 06: Danika Portrait

27th Aug 06: Danika Portrait, originally uploaded by machaggis.

Danika gave me an early birthday present – a new lens for my camera. As I was playing about with it, I grabbed this crazy shot of a tired Danika, which quickly saved me from a dull aphotoperday.
New lens – 50mm f/1.8 Nikkor – my first ever prime lens. Although it auto-focuses it doesn’t zoom, so you have to be more creative. In return it focuses fast, works in less light and is easirt for me to use off Auto (if I ever build up the courage to go there). The 50mm is about 75mm in 35mm ratio, so it makes a perfect “portrait” lens.
{D50, as taken, new lens}

Hanging up my badge

On Friday I hung up my badge at Amazon.com, also turning in those other tools of the trade – pager, VPN, laptop, backup laptop, extra power cable etc, etc.

Yes that’s right, my second tour at the ‘zon is done and dusted and I’m changing job in the next few weeks to go and work for AOL Wireless in the tegic product group – predictive text and cellphones will rule the world.

I am excited and nervous – it’s a big change, and I rarely change jobs so there is a lot for me to learn. It’s also going to be my second experience at a US company, so it will be interesting to compare cultures.

I’ve enjoyed my time at Amazon – they are a good company, and although there are some things I would change, they have a distinct culture and great people. I will be continuing to do my internet shopping there, and geeking out on some of their non-retail recent offerings such as S3. And someday someone may buy something from one my associate links.

With this news, Danika changing job earlier this year and the Clan Waddell due for organic growth, 2006 is certainly working out to be a big year. In general I’m a big fan of the status quo, but sometimes it’s good to attempt to nudge destiny in a direction you choose.

I fear change. Change is good. Change is now.
