12th May 06: Windfarms and Firearms

12th May 06: Windfarms and Firearms, originally uploaded by machaggis.

Between Pasco and Walla Walla there are many windfarms. After trying to take a few photos I realized that the motion of their arms, as well as their setting, is probably what makes them picturesque.
