Bikes!

It must be something about pending parenthood. Not only am I now an active member of the community with the park; I attended a public meeting on the Seattle Bike Master Plan tonight, along with hundreds of others. I didn’t even learn about the meeting until I noticed a sign on my ride in to work this morning.

Highlights: I’m not the only one who hates the exit from the west side of Ballard Bridge heading south – Morgan gave me a reprimand for “graffiti” on the comments poster for phrases such as “fix the death trap”, but in the Q&A someone else said “fix it before we take sledgehammers to it”. Justice, sweet justice.

Lowlights: It’s trying to be all things to all people – trails for the Sunday families who like trails, lanes for the cyclists who are scared of traffic, and shared signs on arterials for riders who believe proactive aggressive is the best defense. They won’t all be implemented fully, so I’m sure we;’ll continue with a solution that isn’t properly joined up. But then, I’m Scottish cynical.
