5th Jan 08: Tartan Tinsel Santa

5th Jan 08: Tartan Tinsel Santa, originally uploaded by machaggis.

It’s “put the tree away” day, and the Tartan Tinsel Santa has to go away again for a year. I think it’s safe to say that D hates this ornament, as it has no sentimental value for her and looks really crappy.

Honestly, it had no prior sentimental value for me either, but sometimes something that’s just so kitsch carries it’s own inertia and awe with it, and now I’m quite fond of it. This year it sat on a picture frame as opposed to the tree itself – I’m unclear if this is a promotion.

Oh, and we’ve aphotoperday‘ed Tartan Tinsel Santa before on the 17th Dec 06.
