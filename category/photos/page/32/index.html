More Photos Than You Ever Want To See…

Or at least, that’s how it appears.

With my daily dalliance with flickr for aphotoperday I have been taking a lot of photos but not been spending the energy in keeping my shutterfly up to date or published. This is lax, because I regularly use shutterfly to make cards and Christmas calendars, alongside prints for family and framing. Although flickr has started doing some of these services I’m pretty investing in my fifth year on shutterfly.

So I’ve uploaded and published all the albums I should have since April this year. Which is a lot (30+) and if you want to look at them feel free to space it out.
