5th May 06: Gutenberg

5th May 06: Gutenberg, originally uploaded by machaggis.

Last night I watched Kristen make her wedding invitations on a 150-year old foot powered printing press. I was most impressed, both mechanically and artistically. I also took loads of photos (once more saving us from a bookshelf, which I had actually taken already after a looong day at work). Although there are some of slightly more artistic value (Morgan and Kristen’s basement is full of photographical delights) I wanted to pay tribute to the motion, the foot, and the wheel.
{D50, as taken}

Update: links to more photos from this session on flickr at More Gutenberg.

2nd May 06: Public Boat Landing, originally uploaded by machaggis.
