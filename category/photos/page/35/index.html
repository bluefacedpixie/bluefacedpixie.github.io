23rd Aug 06: Decemberists at the Zoo

23rd Aug 06: Decemberists at the Zoo, originally uploaded by machaggis.

Nice night out at the Woodland Zoo for some Zoo Tunes (sponsored concerts where the money goes to the zoo – some $900,000 this season). The last band of the summer was our first gig, Portland’s finest: The Decemberists.
These guys pretty much rocked, although the sound was poor towards the back of the meadow, and they even managed to get participation from the usually clinical and comatose Seattle band crowd.
It was our first Zoo Times, and we’ll go back, especially as it seems to be very kid friendly.
{D50, 200mm zoon, handheld, colour tweaked, cropped a little, left a little squint}

16th Aug 06: Sunflower at Night

16th Aug 06: Sunflower at Night, originally uploaded by machaggis.

{D50, macro mode, flash, handheld}
Almost didn’t manage a photo tonight, but the sunflowers saved me. They are still going strong – from the first in July and the latest shot at the start of August.
I replicated the “flash at night” technique from the August shot to capture tonight’s sunflower. Although focused on the centre the off-focus petals on the left look nicely silken.
I also attempted an alternative, handheld, natural light, deliberately blurred (because handheld doesn’t cut it for 60 seconds+) shot, but I’ll include it here rather than posting a second shot for the day.
I call it *16th Aug 06: Impressions of a Sunflower*.

{D50, auto-exposure, handheld, long exposure}
