Two Weddings and a Cupcake

Two sets of our friends were married this weekend gone by. As the weddings were on the same day, at about the same time, Danika and I had to drive between weddings (Golden Gardens to Magnusson Park and back).

Both ceremonies were outside under crystal clear skies with a sliver of moon hanging above the horizon.

Danika and I caught both of the ceremonies, one of the meals and one of the parties. We missed the speeches and first dances on both sides, which is definitely part of the big day, although we think that overall we came out ahead by being able to participate in both events.

Highlights included Bronwyn and Nico (niece and nephew) singing The Rosebuds and Michael Franti for the ceremony, Sean Nelson singing the coda at the other ceremony, an ice cream truck and a band that truly rocked. And everyone seemed to be having fun.

Random photos of Morgan and Kristen’s are here (shutterfly) and Joshua and Shoshanna’s here (also shutterfly) with a slideshow of M&K’s crazy spinning dance here.

I wasn’t playing photographer though, so hopefully better pictures will show up in time.

Stag Night Recap

All in all Morgan’s stag night was a very stately and mature affair.

I made a selection of playing cards with photos of Ballard Pubs on them, and as various people selected from them we made our way to Tarasco, Barking Dog, Bad Alberts, Conor Byrne, The Sloop and Lock and Keel. A tame night really.

We did do the whole trip on bikes, which made for fun – Morgan rode Tracy’s coaster, which was his kind of style.

The photos and cards are available from sale for an outrageous cost of course 🙂

Some photos of the night are on shutterfly, and a crazy *movie* of our ride from the Sloop to the Lock & Keel is available on machaggis.net (ignore the sound, I may overdub later).

The wonders of firefox

I’ve just discovered a cool trick in firefox. If you type into the address (not the seach bar) simple, obvious search terms – they work.

e.g.

  • amazon dvd xmen – goes to the X-Men 2 DVD on Amazon
  • imdb mission impossible 3 – goes to the Mission Impossible 3 imdb.com page
  • wp firefox goes to some stranger’s wordpress blog, but wikipedia firefox takes you to the Mozilla Firefox wikipedia entry (and sometimes wp does do a wikipedia search so it’s not a straight wp=wikipedia/wordpress swap)

Cool.
