I hate iTunes/Airport Express part 1

I’ve resisted this for too long, but many months have passed and I am still unhappy.

I have an airport express that refuses to play music without sometimes skipping (dropouts are the approved internet search term). Somedays it is fine, others it is bad. It’s normally bad late at night and when I have company.

It skips when I stream via ethernet from a Mac Mini to a WRTG54 router to the express, and also when I stream from a Powerbook locally to the WRTG54/Airport express (well with WDS you never know which one you are talking too) and also from the Mac Mini via ethernet/WRTG54/wireless and then to the WRTG54/Airport Express.

I have tried many internet searches and fixes (wireless B, new channels, different encryption…). No joy. So I am proceeding with two tactics a) blog it – who knows what search will give me the perfect solution in my comments (hint, hint) b) I’ll try and borrow another airport express and try that. Who knows – it may be hardware after all.

This is a love/hate/love/love/hate relationship.
