Ride: Back in the saddle

I spent an hour on Saturday cleaning mould from Camelbak hoses as the girls slept in the guest room – q-tips, listerine and coat hangars worked wonders.

It’s a small thing, but biking season is back!.

Today, Forest and I hit Tiger for the first time since October. He was fast, but we were both slow. I was happy I managed to pull my larger-self up the fireroad without any embarrassment beyond an utter reliance on granny gear. Preston was in good conditionand NWTT has some scatterings of fallen brush.

Rolling time was 1hr 36min, which is fine in one respect, and borderline disgraceful in another. The fireroad was 37.

Plenty of time to improve.
