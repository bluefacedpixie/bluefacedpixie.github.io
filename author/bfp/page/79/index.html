Ride: The Last Tiger of ’06 Season

Just over a week ago (before I headed out on some work travel and after the Mac headed to the Genii), Morgan, Forest and I hit Tiger on the last weekend of the season.

Tiger traditionally is an April 15th – October 15th trail, although oft-times inclement or beneficial weather will change those parameters. This year there is a good chance that the season will be extended, but it’s open to the wiles of rangers and cannot be trusted.

I try to make a tradition to ride as close to each end of the season as possible and this year managed the second last day. We rode Iverson first, confirming it’s position as the hardest and most ‘perfect’ trail. It was a nice gray day so I had packed my Nikon and the fixed 50mm lens. I snapped a few shots and will hopefully post them on shutterfly or flickr soon. Morgan was carrying some injuries and after the fireroad climb begged off the downhill trails. That was understandable and Forest and I made short work of Preston and NWTT to join Morgan in the car park. After which some kobe burgers and microbrews at the Rogue Brewery in Issaquah set me in good stead for my overnight flight to Finland.

Bye Tiger, see you next year (although I hope I manage to bike a little more this year!).
